Designed for Cefla Finishing

 

This is a three-axle robot used as the Z axis of  a 3d  finishing’s gantry robot. The two arms are linked and moved by a single servo motor on a straight vertical path . (patented)

This is an industrial gantry robot used for painting furniture. It  is actually produced by CEFLA  Scrl. The five controlled axises (X, Y, Z, b, a ) have  these characteristics:.

From 1986 to 1990, as project leader, I designed parts of  a tobacco Alfa packer machine in SASIB Spa, working on  fast intermittent linkages with and without cams.

From 1990 to 1991, with Ing. Taglioni in Moto Morini and Ducati, I developed  four stroke engines for motorbikes.

 

From 1991 to 1995, as  responsible of  technical office in CEFLA Scrl (the finishing division), I developed and managed  projects for many new machines.

 

And then as consultant I managed other projects like the finishing ovens and  robots.

From 1995 to 1997 I was  technical manager of STM Srl. Inside of the CAM group, STM  designs special machines for industrial automation. STM does work for food, pharmaceutical companies and for other companies inside the CAM group.  STM designs cartoning and wrapping machines, blister machines and special feeding groups for standard automatic machines.
